# Photobooth Open Air

The Photobooth Open Air offers a variety of features, unlimited prints, physical albums for your guests to sign, and a digital platform full of excitement.

- All photobooth packages include a host to ensure a smooth experience
- The props for your event are crafted and carefully selected by our team, to maximize your experience.
- The design of your strips includes your names or your logo, designed by a team of experts in the field.
- Every event includes a 1-2 hour early arrival, setup, takedown, delivery and pick up.
