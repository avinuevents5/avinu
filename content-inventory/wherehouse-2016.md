#### Why we love WhereHouse 2016?

WhereHouse 2016 is perfect for your teens and kids. DJ Levine has performed at this venue for many birthday parties, sweet 16's, quinceaneras, and barmitzvahs. While it's not the ideal venue for weddings, it could be considered among the most creative locations for kids' special events in South Florida. The 3,600 square foot space features floor-to-ceiling wall murals, dozens of handmade light sculptures, illuminated bars, custom-painted tables, chairs, and sofas. Even the floor is painted!
