### DJ Trey

#### Inspiring DJ and MC

Get ready to meet Trey, the ultimate party-starting DJ who lives and breathes the electrifying world of turntables. With over 20 years of non-stop groove, this New Orleans native knows how to rock the house like no other.

Hailing from the city of celebrations, Trey not only earned a degree in Multimedia Production but also a reputation as the life of any party. Picture this: he spun tracks at the wedding of Patrón Tequila big shots, rubbing shoulders with legends like Frank Sinatra's original band. But that's just the tip of the iceberg – Trey's shared the spotlight with global funnyman Kevin Hart, the iconic Ice Cube, and the one and only Alex Monopoly.

His party trail spans epic names like Heineken, Adidas, Ted Baker, and even the tech giants IBM Computers. You might have caught him dropping beats at The Frost Science Museum, Miami's lavish Beverly Hills Hotel, or amid the lush blooms of Fairchild Tropical Botanical Garden. Trey's set the stage on fire at The Walton Manor, The Mayfair Hotel, and The Epic Hotel, not to mention the Miami Supercar Room.

But what truly sets DJ Trey apart is his uncanny ability to vibe with the crowd, coupled with his undying passion for the art of spinning. Get ready for beats that'll make your heart race and a party you'll never forget!

DJ Trey, a New Orleans native with over 20 years in the game, is a master at electrifying turntable performances. From spinning tracks for legends like Frank Sinatra's original band to sharing stages with icons like Kevin Hart and Ice Cube, Trey's resume boasts collaborations with brands like Adidas and IBM, and performances at renowned venues like Miami's Beverly Hills Hotel.
