#### Miami Event Planner and Decorator from Venezuela

Zais Cortez, a Venezuelan native, has been deeply immersed in the art of event planning from a young age. Starting with organizing intimate birthday gatherings for loved ones in her teenage years, her passion drove her to pursue formal education in event planning alongside her university studies in 2005.

Recognizing the importance of professional accreditation, Zais obtained certification as a Wedding and Event Planner, solidifying her commitment to excellence.

Now based in Miami, Zais continues to expand her expertise with certification from the prestigious Longevity Wedding and Event Planning Institute. With a portfolio boasting over 500 weddings and events, and many of those alongside Mark Levine and Avinu Music, her unwavering dedication and mission is evident: "To deliver unparalleled joy through exceptional event experiences."
