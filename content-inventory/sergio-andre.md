#### Sergio Andre Garcia

Hailing from Venezuela, Sergio Andre is an extraordinary multilingual singer, captivating audiences with his enchanting performances in Spanish, English, and the captivating realm of Jewish music. As Mark Levine's right-hand collaborator, Sergio has seamlessly blended his talents with Mark's expertise, creating harmonies that transcend language barriers.

With a global following of over 50,000 dedicated fans, Sergio has made a resounding impact in the music industry. His soul-stirring love tunes resonate deeply with listeners worldwide, leaving an indelible mark on their hearts. Through platforms like Spotify and Youtube, Sergio's melodic expressions of love have reached and touched the lives of countless individuals.

In addition to his musical pursuits, Sergio Andre has expanded his creative horizons into the realm of wedding videography. Collaborating closely with the esteemed Levine Events Team, he has skillfully captured the essence and magic of numerous events. Sergio's artistic vision and attention to detail result in captivating wedding videos that become cherished keepsakes for couples and their families.

Sergio's versatile talents, combined with his passion for artistic expression, have allowed him to carve a unique path in the realm of music and videography. With each performance and film project, he showcases a remarkable ability to evoke emotions and transport audiences into realms of beauty and love.

As Sergio Andre continues to evolve as a multilingual musical virtuoso, his unwavering commitment to his craft shines through in every endeavor. Whether serenading audiences with his soulful vocals or capturing cherished moments on film, Sergio's artistry illuminates the beauty that resides within the human experience.

Immerse yourself in the captivating world of Sergio Andre's music and videography, and embark on a journey that will leave you spellbound and inspired. Witness the fusion of multilingual melodies and visual storytelling as Sergio masterfully weaves his talents into an unforgettable tapestry of artistic brilliance.
