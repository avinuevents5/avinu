### Uplights

## Lighting

We know that the importance of your wedding day surpasses that of any other celebration. That's why it is our priority to ensure your special day comes to life in exactly the way you imagined.

## Intelligent dance floor Lighting

Revitalize the dance floor with a novel energy that's impossible to resist. Intelligent dance lights move and shift to the beat of the music, reinventing the light-and-sound relationship on the dance floor.

## Bistro Lighting

This ultra-festive yet undeniably romantic detail can revamp any space, indoors or out. Bistro lighting is just the thing to compliment a night under the stars, or to expand upon the warmth that fills an indoor venue.

## Show Mapping

Show mapping projection technology turns any object into a display surface for video projection. Fill the room with colours and designs to add a modern wow-factor to your event.

## Gobo Monogram

Let us create a customized logo of your event. Give us the name of your mitzvah-boy or mitzvah-girl, the initials of the newly-weds, or simply hand us your existing company logo, and we'll project it onto the dance floor and the walls of the venue. This special touch is the perfect way to put the guests of honor in the spotlight.

## Pinspot lighting

Pinspot lighting highlights the details that you've put so much thought into selecting. Enhance your flower displays, centerpieces, cake, or sweetheart table to up the elegance of your special day.
