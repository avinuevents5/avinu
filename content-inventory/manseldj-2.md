# Mark LevinE

Mark Levine is the head DJ and founder of Levine Events, where his heart and energy have created the most spectacular events worldwide.

From a very early age, Mark was attracted to sounds and languages, becoming fluent in Spanish, French, Portuguese, Italian, and now improving his Hebrew. His angelic voice sets a magical setting when he sings at Jewish wedding ceremonies.

Committed to pursuing a career in music, he was taken by storm to Los Angeles, Brazil, France under the Walt Disney Studios, Mexico with TV Azteca, and finally to Miami where this production company Levine Events was built.

Mark has had the opportunity to perform for: Pepsi, Adidas, Nintendo, Univision, MTV with majors stars like Robin Thicke, Olga Tanon, Missy Elliot, Martha Sanchez, David Beckham, and more.

As a DJ, he opened at the concert of latin star Juanes, single-handedly performed for "The Rock" Dwaine Johnson and took the stage in the appearance of Andrea Bocelli in Miami.
