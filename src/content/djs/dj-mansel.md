DJ Mansel has been DJ'ing for over a decade. Young and charismatic, elegant, well-spoken and a great listener, he acquired a taste for mixing music and entertaining crowds at weddings. Since then he has expanded his skills to corporate events, sweet sixteens, quinceañeras and much more.

With more than 400 events under his belt and years of experience, he has the unique skill of reading a crowd and selecting the right tune to spark excitement on the dance floor. Creating fusion energy by matching beats — honed through years of DJ'ing — makes for the best kind of event imaginable.
