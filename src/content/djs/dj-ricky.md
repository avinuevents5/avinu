DJ Ricky is a native Miami DJ, MC and singer with over a decade of experience. Known for his energetic personality at weddings, corporate events and bar mitzvahs, DJ Ricky is an essential part of a successful event.

Though he has been passionate about music since childhood, he began his music career as a DJ in 2012 — playing private parties, family and neighborhood functions while singing at his local church. After a few years, he started working professionally as a DJ and singer for weddings and corporate events.

After about 300 multicultural events under his belt, his intrigue and love for spinning and making crowds dance has not changed. His goal is to keep all his clients happy and to have everyone enjoy his music on the dance floor. He specializes in R&B, Hip Hop, Top 40s, Caribbean and so much more — ready to give you a party you will never forget.
