Nestled in the heart of Boca Raton, Florida, The Addison is more than just a venue; it's a cherished piece of local history and a beacon of timeless elegance. Built in 1925 by renowned architect Addison Mizner, The Addison carries a rich history and charm that echoes back to the elegance of the 1920s.

Couples rave about The Addison's stunning architecture and scenery, attentive staff, and delicious food. From the moment you step onto the property, you'll be transported to a bygone era of glamour and sophistication. The Addison is the perfect place to celebrate your special day in style.

If you're looking for a truly unique and unforgettable wedding venue, The Addison is the perfect choice.
