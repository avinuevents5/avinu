Hyatt Centric Las Olas is a wedding venue located in Fort Lauderdale, Florida. Just a few blocks from the city's yacht-lined riverfront, this hotel is situated in the heart of the downtown area. When celebrating here, couples and their loved ones will have access to the city's vibrant entertainment district and local art.

Hyatt Centric Las Olas offers a number of versatile event spaces for you to choose from. The Stranahan Ballroom features a modern design, blue uplighting, and ocean-inspired chandeliers, reflecting the city's culture and seaside setting. The 3,000-square-foot space can accommodate up to 150 guests for your wedding celebrations. Just outside of the ballroom's glass doors is the Elev8 Rooftop. If you'd like to exchange vows in the warm Florida air, you are welcome to host your ceremony with up to 150 people here. The rooftop contains a pool and an altar space featuring greenery, along with chairs and aisle markers.

After you and your partner say "I do," you can continue to celebrate outside for your reception. Your loved ones can enjoy drinks and hors d'oeuvres while relaxing on the modern lounge seating or at the multi-colored cocktail tables. The Elev8 Rooftop is bordered by leafy palm trees for a truly Florida setting in the city's center. Before the celebration, you and your partner are invited to prepare in separate get-ready rooms with your wedding parties. When the festivities draw to a close, you and your guests can rest in any of the hotel's 238 rooms.

#### Services Offered

The team at Hyatt Centric Las Olas looks forward to supporting you during your special occasion. They will be there to set up and clean up your celebration no matter the event space you choose. The on-site culinary team is happy to prepare a variety of menu choices for you and your loved ones to enjoy. Wedding packages offer all-inclusive plated dinner services, designed to wow your guests during each course. If you prefer a buffet or reception dinner service, the catering team is happy to create a buffet display, with options ranging from Japanese sushi and dim sum, to ceviche and slow-cooked pulled pork. You can also request one of their talented chefs to serve Asian-noodle, sautéed-shrimp, or fish-taco stations. Bar services are also available, including a wide selection of house liquor.
