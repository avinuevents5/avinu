Benvenuto Restaurant's historic 1929 Mizner-inspired building. Breathtaking Tuscan-influenced architecture sets the scene for your happily ever after, while meticulously maintained gardens bursting with vibrant blooms create a fairytale-like atmosphere.

Celebrate your love under the warm glow of string lights twinkling in the romantic outdoor European courtyard, or choose one of the beautifully appointed indoor spaces for a more intimate gathering. As the evening unfolds, step outside to the charming courtyard, where laughter and joy fill the air as you celebrate surrounded by your loved ones.

Benvenuto Restaurant provides a truly unforgettable setting for your special day.
